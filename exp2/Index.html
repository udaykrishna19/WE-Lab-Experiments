Welcome to Mickey's World

"Mickey Mouse" is an iconic cartoon character created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks in 1928. Debuting in the animated short film "Steamboat Willie," Mickey quickly became a beloved symbol of The Walt Disney Company. With his cheerful demeanor, distinctive appearance, and adventurous spirit, Mickey has starred in countless animated shorts, feature films, television shows, and comics, captivating audiences worldwide. As a global cultural icon, Mickey Mouse continues to inspire and entertain generations, embodying the timeless magic and imagination of Disney entertainment.
