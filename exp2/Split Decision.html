Split Decision

"Split Decision" is an episode from the Mickey Mouse television series that showcases Mickey and his pals, Goofy and Donald, embarking on an unforgettable road trip adventure. The trio sets out on a journey with a single goal in mind: to visit the spectacular Split Decision Canyon. However, their plans take an unexpected turn when they encounter a series of comical mishaps and obstacles along the way. From navigating treacherous terrain to facing off against wily adversaries, Mickey and his friends must rely on their wit, teamwork, and quick thinking to overcome each challenge and reach their destination. Filled with humor, excitement, and heartwarming moments, "Split Decision" highlights the enduring camaraderie and adventurous spirit of Mickey Mouse and his beloved companions, offering viewers a delightful and entertaining escapade from start to finish.
